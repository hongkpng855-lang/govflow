## 目標問題
**"How to register a Hong Kong company?"** — https://www.quora.com/How-do-you-register-a-Hong-Kong-company

## 建議答案（英文，自然 tone）

**Registering a Hong Kong private limited company is surprisingly straightforward — here's the actual step-by-step, including what you can do yourself vs. what you might need help with.**

### Step 1: Choose your company name
- English name, Chinese name, or both
- Check availability on the Companies Registry e-Search (free): https://www.e-services.cr.gov.hk/
- Names can't be identical or too similar to existing ones

### Step 2: Prepare the documents
You'll need:
- **Incorporation Form (NNC1)** — for company incorporation
- **Business Registration Application (IRBR1)** — for the tax registration
- **Articles of Association** — standard version available from CR

The total government fee is **HK$1,720** (HK$1,545 for incorporation + HK$175 for business registration). You can file everything online at the CR e-Services Portal.

### Step 3: Get your certificates
Within 1-2 working days you'll receive:
- Certificate of Incorporation
- Business Registration Certificate
- Company seal (optional since 2014 Ordinance change)

### Step 4: Post-incorporation must-dos
Don't stop here. You also need to:
- **Appoint a company secretary** (mandatory under Cap. 622)
- **Get a registered office address in Hong Kong**
- **Open a corporate bank account**
- **Issue share certificates** to shareholders
- **Maintain statutory registers** (directors, shareholders, SCR if applicable)

### Common mistakes first-timers make
1. **Skipping the company secretary requirement** — you can't legally operate without one
2. **Not understanding stamp duty on share transfers** — if you issue shares later, both buyer and seller pay 0.1% each
3. **Ignoring annual compliance** — Annual Return (NAR1) + audit + tax return every year

### Free resources you can use
Rather than paying a service provider for every little thing, there are free tools that help with the paperwork:

- **NAR1 Annual Return** — free PDF tool: https://esgov.org/nar1-generator/
- **Share transfer documents** (Instrument of Transfer, Sold Note, Letter of Transferee) — free PDF generators: https://esgov.org/shareholder-transfer/
- **Special Resolution for name changes** — free fillable PDF: https://esgov.org/company-name-change/
- **SCR (Significant Controllers Register)** — free compliance guides: https://esgov.org/significant-controllers-register/
- **Audit Report** — free PDF generator: https://esgov.org/audit-report-generator/

Full compliance checklist: https://esgov.org/blog/香港有限公司每年合規全面Checklist

### When you should hire a professional
- Complex share structures with multiple classes
- Cross-border tax planning
- VC/PE investments with complicated terms
- If you simply don't have time to manage compliance yourself

But for straightforward single-shareholder companies, you can handle 80% of the compliance yourself with the right tools. The expensive part isn't incorporation — it's the ongoing annual compliance.

## 建議答案（中文，如果條問題係中文）

**喺香港開有限公司，其實一啲都唔複雜 — 自己搞都得，唔使俾幾千蚊俾人賺。**

### 步驟好簡單：
1. **諗公司名** — 上 CR e-Search 免費 check 名
2. **填 NNC1 + IRBR1 表** — CR 網上系統一次過交，政府費 HK$1,720
3. **等 1-2 個工作日** — 收到公司註冊證書 + 商業登記證

### 開完公司之後嘅重要事項：
- 一定要有 **公司秘書**（法例規定）
- 要有 **香港註冊地址**
- 開 **公司銀行戶口**
- 準備 **股票證明書**
- 保持 **法定登記冊**（董事、股東、SCR）

### 每年要做嘅嘢：
- 週年申報表（NAR1）— 有免費工具幫手填：https://esgov.org/nar1-generator/
- 審計報告（核數）— 免費 PDF 生成器：https://esgov.org/audit-report-generator/
- 報稅
- 重要控制人登記冊（SCR）更新

### 免費工具幫你慳錢：
市面上好多工具係免費嘅，唔使下下俾錢服務商：
- 股份轉讓文件（Instrument of Transfer / Sold Note）：https://esgov.org/shareholder-transfer/

**簡單嘅公司結構，自己搞掂 80% 冇問題。最貴嘅唔係開公司，係每年嘅 compliance。**
